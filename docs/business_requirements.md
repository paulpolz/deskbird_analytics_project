Take-Home Assignment - Sr. Growth Analyst

Scenario: You’ve just joined deskbird as the Sr. Growth Analyst. The Growth team needs a reliable set of metrics and data models to understand the end-to-end customer journey. You are provided fictional raw datasets:

- Paid ads data (impressions, clicks, spend, conversions)
- CRM pipeline (leads, opportunities, closed won/lost)
- Product usage events (logins, seat utilization, feature adoption)

Your task:

1. Design a high-level data model (e.g., schema diagram, dbt DAG sketch, or clear table relationships) that connects these datasets in a scalable way.
2. Define 5 key growth metrics that you would rely on to measure acquisition, conversion, and retention.
3. Use the fictional dataset to surface exploratory insights that could help shape deskbird’s Growth strategy.
4. Present your work in a slide deck, showing how your model and metrics enable actionable insights.

Expected output:

- 6–10 pages long slide deck including:
  - Data model diagram
  - Defined metrics with rationale
  - Exploratory insights with visualization(s)
  - Recommendations
  - Codebase in Python (e.g. ipynb files), SQL or others showing how you came to your
    findings.

Tips:

- Aim for clarity and stakeholder-readability in your slides.
- You do not need to cover every single column, focus on what’s most impactful.
- Feel free to aggregate or manipulate data as you see fit
- Focus on reproducibility
